The Tunisian Judo Federation was pleased to hold a training day on the theme of
"Resilience, Character Strengths and Emotional Intelligence", led by the expert
Dr Lobna Cherif and hosted by Mr Abdelkarim Mallat, a federal member of the
Tunisian Judo Federation.

The day was a genuine development opportunity for our coaches, technical staff
and everyone active in Tunisian judo. Beyond the technical side, sporting
performance also rests on human qualities: the ability to manage emotions, to
face difficulties, and to bring out each person's strengths of character.

In sport, these skills are essential — they help coaches understand their
athletes better, adapt how they support them, and build the confidence,
motivation and cohesion of their teams, all while developing a positive and
inspiring form of leadership.

The Tunisian Judo Federation warmly thanks Dr Lobna Cherif for the quality of her
contribution, and Mr Abdelkarim Mallat for hosting a day rich in exchange and
learning.

Together, let us keep training capable coaches, resilient athletes, and leaders
ready to meet the challenges ahead.
