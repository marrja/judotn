Under a glorious summer sky, on the beautiful shore of the town of Soliman, the
beach judo tournament took place before a notable turnout and great enthusiasm
from the participants — an occasion where the joy of the sport blended with the
beauty of nature and a spirit of friendship and cooperation.

The tournament drew every age group, in an atmosphere of joy, encouragement and
positive exchange, confirming once again that judo is not merely a competitive
sport but a genuine school of education and character-building.

Our thanks and appreciation go to the technical and organising staff, to all the
coaches and participants, and to everyone who helped make this fine sporting
event a success — a reflection of the efforts made to spread judo and root its
noble values across every region.
