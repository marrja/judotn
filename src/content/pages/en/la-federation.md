## Remit

The Tunisian Judo Federation (FTJUDO) governs the practice of judo and its associated
disciplines — aikido and kendo — throughout Tunisia.

## The moral code

Judo rests on eight values carried on the tatami and beyond: politeness, courage,
sincerity, honour, modesty, respect, self-control and friendship.
