# Migration WordPress → Astro — what changed and why

Companion to [`phase1-audit.md`](phase1-audit.md), which describes the old site.
This document is the record of what was carried over, what was rebuilt, and what
was deliberately left behind.

---

## Scope decision

The source database contained **two websites**. The install began as _AATUJA —
Association d'Amitié Tunisie-Japon_ (`tunisia-japan.org`) and was partially
rebranded in June 2024 into the _Fédération Tunisienne de Judo_. The rebrand was
never finished: the desktop navigation served Judo menus while the `mobile` menu
location still served the old AATUJA menu, so visitors saw a different site
depending on their device.

**Decision: build the Judo Federation site only, and _delete_ the AATUJA content
rather than redirect it.** Those URLs (`/about-us/`, `/members-board/`,
`/sample-page/*`) now return the custom 404 page. This is deliberate: a 301 to an
unrelated page is a soft-404 that search engines penalise, and it misleads anyone
following an old link. Gone is gone.

## Content transformations

**Page-builder markup stripped.** Every page's real content lived in
`postmeta._elementor_data` as a JSON widget tree, with a flattened HTML snapshot
mirrored into `post_content`. Neither was usable directly. Content was read from
the widget tree and re-authored as Markdown; all Elementor `<div>` scaffolding,
inline styles and `data-*` attributes are gone.

**Shortcodes resolved.** The 10 Documents posts had bodies consisting solely of
`[pdf-embedder url="..."]`. That is now a typed `pdf` field with a validated
`/documents/` path and a byte size, rendered as a download button plus an
on-demand preview.

**Titles de-shouted.** Six document titles were stored in all caps
(`RÈGLES D'ARBITRAGE DE LA FIJ`). They are now sentence case with acronyms
preserved (`Règles d'arbitrage de la FIJ`).

**Empty bodies acknowledged.** The 5 news posts and 1 announcement had _no body
text_ in WordPress — the featured image was the entire post. That is preserved
faithfully: title, date and image. Nothing was invented to fill the space.

**SEO written fresh.** Yoast was installed but never configured — every one of
the 13 indexable rows had an empty title and empty description. There was
nothing to migrate, so titles and meta descriptions are authored per page. All
27 pages now have a non-empty title, a description over 50 characters, and a
canonical URL.

## Verification results

`npm run build && npm test` — all green.

```
legacy permalinks   17 legacy URLs, all resolve
redirect targets    6 redirects, all targets exist
deleted paths       5 AATUJA paths confirmed absent, 404 handler present
internal links      762 links checked, 0 dead
WordPress residue   27 pages scanned, 0 shortcodes / escaped HTML / old domains
required files      sitemap, rss, robots, 404, .htaccess present; 10 PDFs
per-page head       title, description, canonical, one h1, alt on every img
```

The suite was mutation-tested: injecting a dead link, a `[pdf-embedder]`
shortcode and an `<img>` without `alt` produced three failures, confirming the
checks actually fire.

### Image optimisation

All 7 content images are converted to WebP at build time and served with a
3-width `srcset`. Originals averaged 121 kB; the narrow-viewport variant now
averages **14 kB** — roughly a 90% reduction. Below-the-fold images are
`loading="lazy"`; the lead card and article headers are `eager`.

### Accessibility

Every colour pair in the design was computed against WCAG:

| Pair                       | Ratio   |     |
| -------------------------- | ------- | --- |
| Body text on background    | 16.95:1 | AAA |
| Body text on card          | 15.86:1 | AAA |
| Muted text on background   | 5.70:1  | AA  |
| Accent links on background | 5.51:1  | AA  |
| Accent on card             | 5.16:1  | AA  |
| White on the crimson band  | 4.67:1  | AA  |
| Footer legal text          | 4.58:1  | AA  |

**Worst pair: 4.58:1** — everything clears AA for normal text.

Two issues found and fixed during verification:

1. **Footer legal text was 2.98:1** (`#5f5c58` on the darker footer ground) —
   below AA. Lifted to `#7d7975`, now 4.58:1.
2. **The brand crimson `#ac1e36` fails on a near-black ground** (2.9:1). Rather
   than abandon the brand colour, the palette splits it: `--accent` (`#e0243f`)
   for fills and large display type, `--accent-text` (`#ff3b52`, 5.51:1) for
   anything read at body size.

Also in place: semantic `<header>/<nav>/<main>/<footer>` landmarks, a skip link,
`aria-label` on every nav, `aria-current="page"` on the active menu item, visible
focus rings, `prefers-reduced-motion` honoured, `lang="fr"` on every page,
exactly one `<h1>` per page, and `alt` on every image.

### Known, accepted

- **685 kB of unreferenced JPEGs in `dist/_astro/`.** Astro emits the original
  alongside the WebP derivatives. They are referenced by nothing (verified: 0
  references) so no visitor ever downloads them; it is deploy-artifact bloat
  only.
- **`reglement-arbitrage-IJF-aout-2020.pdf` is 9.9 MB.** It is the FIJ's own
  file, republished as-is. The document page shows the size on the button and
  loads the preview only on request, so nobody downloads it by accident.
